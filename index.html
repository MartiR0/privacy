DailyTrend Bot – Privacy Policy

Last updated: 06 July 2025

This Privacy Policy explains what information the “DailyTrend” chatbot (the “Bot”) collects via the Instagram Graph API, how we use it, and the choices you have.

1. Information We Collect

  • Instagram user identifiers (IDs) provided by Meta*
  • Content of incoming Direct Messages, comments, and mentions
  • Timestamps associated with each message or comment
  • Server log data (IP address, user‑agent) automatically collected by our hosting providers (GitHub Pages & AWS)

2. How We Use the Data

  • To generate automated replies to your messages and comments via the IG API
  • To analyse aggregated, anonymised interaction statistics and improve reply quality
  • To maintain audit logs required by the Meta Platform Policies

3. Legal Basis

Processing is based on our legitimate interest in providing timely, automated support to followers of the @dailytrend.sale account.

4. Retention

Message and comment content is retained for no longer than 30 days, after which it is automatically deleted. Server logs are stored for up to 90 days.

5. Sharing

We do not sell or share personal information except:

  • With hosting providers (AWS, GitHub Pages) acting as data processors
  • When required to comply with lawful requests from public authorities

6. Your Rights

If you are located in the EU/EEA, UK or California, you may request access, correction, or erasure of your data. Please email us at support@dailytrend.sale.

7. Cookies

The Bot itself does not use cookies. GitHub Pages and Cloudflare may set technical cookies necessary for security and performance.
